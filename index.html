Between two worlds

Hi. I'm Lino, a software dev from Manta, Ecuador.

Ever since I left my hometown for studies in 2013, I've found myself living between two worlds, and it remains true to this day.

This website attempts to capture that sensation.

Dev projects The Predictive Maintenance Game

Released in July 2024

A simulation game inspired by the likes of Game Dev Tycoon, where your goal is to maximize profit & lifespan of your machine, with sensors & predictive analytics at your disposal.

Built with FastAPI & Svelte. Uses MQTT and a regression model under the hood.
